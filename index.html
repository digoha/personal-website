About Me

Hello, my name is Rodrigo Herstler Albernaz, and I am a Digital Designer with a Bachelor's degree in Game Development from Centro Universitário Senac.

I currently work at HighGrounder Studio as a 3D generalist, creating optimized 3D models for games and animations.

I am passionate about 3D art, design, multimedia production, and game development.

Software and tools I use: Adobe (After Effects, Audition, Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Premiere Pro), Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word), Unity, Unreal, Canva, Figma, ChatGPT, Visual Studio Code, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, ClickUp, Trello, Notion, GitHub, Blender, Marmoset Toolbag, Maya, ZBrush.

I am always looking to enhance my knowledge and skills in technology and design, aiming to create unique and impactful experiences for people.
